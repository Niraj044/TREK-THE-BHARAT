    Overview

    Pushkar: Pushkar is a holy town located in the state of Rajasthan, known for its sacred lake, ghats, and temples. It's also famous for the Pushkar Camel Fair, held annually. Here's what you can do in Pushkar:

    1. Pushkar Lake: Visit the sacred Pushkar Lake and take part in the ritual of bathing in its holy waters. The lake is surrounded by ghats and temples, creating a serene atmosphere.

    2. Brahma Temple: Pay a visit to the Brahma Temple, one of the few temples dedicated to Lord Brahma in the world.

    3. Pushkar Camel Fair: If your visit coincides with the Pushkar Camel Fair (usually held in November), don't miss the opportunity to witness this colorful extravaganza, where thousands of camels are bought and sold, and various cultural events take place.

    4. Savitri Temple: Take a hike or cable car ride to the Savitri Temple, located atop a hill, to enjoy panoramic views of Pushkar town and the surrounding desert landscape.

    5. Holi Celebrations: Celebrate Holi in Pushkar with enthusiasm, as the town comes alive with colors, music, and festivities. You can join in the fun with locals and travelers alike, smearing colors and enjoying traditional sweets.

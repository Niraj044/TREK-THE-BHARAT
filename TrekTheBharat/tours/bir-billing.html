    Overview

    Bir Billing, located in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India, is renowned as one of the world's best paragliding destinations. Here's a brief description:

    1. *Bir:* Known for its Tibetan Colony, Bir exudes a tranquil ambience with monasteries like Chokling and Palpung Sherabling. The town is culturally rich, offering a blend of traditional and modern influences.

    2. *Billing:* Situated at a higher altitude, Billing serves as the take-off site for paragliders. The vast meadows and clear skies make it an ideal spot for thrilling paragliding adventures.

    Itinerary
    DAY 1: BIR SIGHTSEEING | STAY
    • Reach Bir in the morning. Check-in at stay.
    • Freshen up, Take some rest.
    • After this head out for sightseeing. Visit the Monastery and famous Bangoru Waterfall also known as Hidden Waterfall. Visit Bir Local market for some shopping and explore local cafes for some Tibetan cuisine.
    • Dinner and overnight stay at Bir. 

     

    DAY 2: PARAGLIDING | DEPARTURE
    • Wake up to a beautiful morning and set yourself up for the flight of your life.
    • After breakfast, start your journey to Billing for paragliding (expense not included). Reach Bir by afternoon.
    • Chill at cafes or go for mountain biking (not included in the package).
    • By evening head back to Delhi/Chandigarh with some amazing trip memories.
